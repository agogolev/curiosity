Prehistoric Milestones from a Layman's View

It is hard to gain intuition about large spans of time. When it comes to anything more than 200 years, we tend to think about it as just a "very long time ago." I put together this amateur prehistoric timeline to get a better intuition about Neolithic and Paleolithic times, with milestones that amused me.

The left chart shows the temperature delta compared to our current average temperature. This data series comes from the Vostok ice cores, so it is less relevant for the Northern Hemisphere.
